The Framework · The B2B Marketing Engine

Turn enterprise momentum into adoption, pipeline, and trust.

B2B marketing's job in APAC is to convert OpenAI's enterprise momentum into adoption and pipeline — across three audiences and five full-funnel engines, tuned to how APAC actually buys: through trust, regulation, partners, and local proof. Partner marketing is one of those five engines, built out in full on the Partner Strategy page.

01 · The remit

Three outcomes B2B marketing owns.

Everything the function does ladders up to these three. They're the test for whether a program earns its place.

Outcome

Enterprise adoption

Move enterprises from awareness to deployment at scale across priority markets.

Outcome

Pipeline growth

Generate and accelerate sourced and influenced pipeline alongside a sales-led GTM.

Outcome

Long-term trust

Build durable credibility in trust-sensitive, regulated APAC enterprise environments.

02 · The audiences

Three audiences, marketed differently.

The B2B remit spans three distinct audiences — each with its own buyer, proof, and motion.

Audience

Enterprise

CIOs, Chief AI Officers, and regulated industries. Win on trust, security, and local proof.

Audience

Partner-led

The ecosystem that extends reach and delivery. The Partner Strategy is the engine for this audience.

Audience

Developer-adjacent business

Technical buyers and builders who shape and influence business adoption.

Full-funnel, across five program engines.

The remit runs the full funnel through five program areas. Each plays a different role — and each maps to creating demand or capturing it.
